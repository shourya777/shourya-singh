About

I’m an Data Engineer focused on building dependable data products: ingestion, transformation, modelling, and delivery into BI and analytics workflows. I work at the intersection of engineering and analytics — shipping datasets that are structured, tested, and documented so teams can self-serve reliable insights.

I’m comfortable operating across ambiguity and multiple stakeholders: defining business logic, tracing data flows end-to-end, enforcing quality standards, and optimising performance and cost in cloud warehouse environments.

Analytics Engineering Approach

  • Model-first design: define facts, dimensions, and business logic explicitly
  • Quality by default: validation checks (nullability, uniqueness, freshness, schema drift)
  • Performance-aware SQL: optimise transformations for scalable cloud execution
  • Governance & documentation: clear definitions, ownership, and reusable patterns
  • Cross-team collaboration: translate requirements into production-ready datasets
